So how does PyraSpace work?

Buyer                                                                                        

  • Navigate and search our effiecient platform for item's you'd like to purchase.
  • Sign Up/ Log in to purchase an item
  • Your details will be sent to the owner of the item who will contact you for further communication

Seller                                                                                        

  • Create a PyraSpace account to begin advertising your items.
  • PyraSpace charges a once-off flat fee of R15, sellers can't receive buyer details without settling this fee
  • PyraSpace does not take percentage cuts of your products
  • Once ad-fee is settled and a buyer is found, their contact details will be sent to you
